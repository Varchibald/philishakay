Hi there! Thanks for being here.

My name is Philisha and this website is meant to serve as an ever-evolving work-in-progress portfolio / archive of my work.

As you'll see once you click on the "projects" tab, the thematic interest of my diverse endeavours often lies in the intersections of identity, technology and art.

Through the discursive queering and applicatory re-imagination of the realm of the Digital, I envision emancipatory and participative presents.

In particular, I am intrigued by the potential of (digital) imaginaries and technologies as subversive tools for the creative (de-)construction of -self as well as the aesthetic re-conceptualisation of gendered (corpo-)reality.

I am based in Berlin and do project based freelance work in different functions, such as (digital) creator, curator and facilitator - often through the dgtl fmnsm collective, which I am a proud member of.
