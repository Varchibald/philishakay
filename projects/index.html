FACE
i am the producer and host of the workshop series dgtl fmsnm FACE, executed in cooperation with HAU hebbel am ufer.

my responsibilies include curating the episodes, developing their content, moderating them with different co-hosts as well as organising the technical setup and managing resources & budgets.

the workshops address the ever evolving political role of the human FACE from different discursive and technological angles. they are executed as an hybrid format with both a live audience and online participants.

ep01_FACE your cover (september 2020)
ep02_FACE your filter (october 2020)
ep03_FACE your way (november 2020)
ep04_FACE your bodies (february 2021)
ep05_FACE your features (april 2021)
HOT MESS

for the dgtl fmnsm episode HOT MESS, part of the HAU hebbel am ufer spy on me #2 festival, i worked as an assistant producer.

my responsibilities included communicating with both HAU and involved artist, managing budgets and contracts, designing and publishing the social media strategie and co-hosting GLEAM, a desktopshow that served as the centerpiece of the episode, featured different artists and art forms and was live-streamed on YouTube.

the whole program was successfully rescheduled to happen online in a matter of days in march 2020 right as the corona virus was beginning to spread throughout the world.
OWN

my artistic practice includes performances that i would have previously called “drag” and now describe as externalized introspection because they serve me as a way to reflect and display my own perceived performativity in society built around binaries.

they center around topics of transition and identity finding and also aim to serve as political commentary on issues such as topic masculinity and cisnormativity. my inspiration often comes from music video pop culture.

my latest performance is available to be viewed on my instagram profile (igtv)
